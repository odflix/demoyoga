About Yoga Samvidaa

Yoga Samvidaa – Wisdom of Yoga, Healing from within

Seeking a remedy for restoring good health is crucial as it rectifies the disruption caused to physical, mental, and spiritual activities, while recognizing the therapeutic power within oneself. At Yoga Samvidaa, we believe in harnessing this inner power to promote holistic well-being by following yogas two important principles.

Pranayama

Breathing techniques. Breath leads your movements and helps relax and stretch your muscles and tendons. Breath is also the guide to calm the mind.

Asanas

Poses that exercise the front, back, and sides of the body equally. Asanas create a full range of movements, such as horizontal and vertical expansion.
